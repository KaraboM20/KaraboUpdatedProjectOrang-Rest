Why Choose Orange Restaurant?

We've Got It All

Wedding & Events
Above and beyond the most spectacular view over the city and its surrounding valleys, we take pride in our immaculate service, our fine selection of wines and modern cuisine, suitable to just about any palate. We offer an exclusivity that is rare to the area, and therefore, we endeavor to keep the weddings held at the restaurant exclusive too. Conveniently, you can have both the ceremony and reception at the restaurant, as well as have your guests stay over at the guest house which shares the same premises as the restaurant.
Conferencing
We offer a modern executive boardroom with a maximum capacity of 50 delegates. The facility is fully equipped with the latest computerised technology ensuring high-quality presentations. Our Conference Facility comes standard with:
-Drop down screen and data projector
-Wi-Fi Connectivity
-Flipchart, notepads and pens
-Cordless Microphone
-Podium
-Sound System
-Water, mints and cordials
-Audio Visual System; just bring your laptop.
The Mandarin Room
A private dining room inside Orange Restaurant. The Mandarin Room is located within the restaurant itself and is perfectly suited to small, private affairs of six people and larger, more extravagant affairs for up to forty people!
Accommodating up to 40 people for a seated lunch or dinner, or up to 60 guests for a cinema styled conference or networking session, the Mandarin Room offers privacy, space and state of the Art conference facilities for your event.
